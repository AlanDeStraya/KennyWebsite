About Kenny

Kenny's original songs have been heard across his native Canada since 2010, including in a musical adaptation of classic Greek comedy 'Lysistrata' where he wrote his first full original score for a musical, Daniel MacIvor's 'Marion Bridge' (Armagh Sifton-Price Award* - Best Original Score & Theme Song), and his award-winning performance as Steve in 'Henry' (Nepean Little Theatre Award** - Best Actor In A Leading Role). With the help of his partner Rafael Maia (@capitalraf), Kenny performed to online crowds during the beginning of the COVID19 pandemic in his co-created 'Physical Distancing Sessions', amassing more than 50,000 listeners and viewers worldwide. The sessions garnered Kenny and Rafael the 'Shine On' award from Orpheus Musical Theatre Society, recognizing their artistic contributions to bettering pandemic life in Canada's capital of Ottawa, Ontario. His original songs range from healing and heartbreak, funny and feeling, to tragedy and triumph.
